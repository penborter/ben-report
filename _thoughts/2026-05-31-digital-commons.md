**Prosocial positive externalities of ignorance.** In other words, who reaps the benefits of a question on the internet that gets asked and answered?

I'd read about similar things before with the walled gardens of Discord etc., where discussions that used to be out in the public web now take place on site untouched by search engine indexers.

But now the answers people get in Google searches are generated uniquely for them. Not a common link with the same answer for everyone, and potentially all the related, adjacent [context](/notes/context).
